Overview

The Project

Natural Beauty is an e-commerce website and mobile app that sells vegan, cruelty-free cosmetics with refillable containers and an AI-powered product recommendation feature. Our goal was to design a purchase experience that is easier, clearer, and more trustworthy for users.

Discover

Understanding the Problem

Competitive Analysis

We gathered essential data by comparing approximately 70 cosmetics websites across multiple dimensions. This allowed us to identify patterns and gaps across the market.

Competitive analysis of cosmetics websites

User Interviews

After deriving insights from competitive analysis, we interviewed 10 potential users using the 5W+H framework. Key demands that emerged:

  • Buying vegan and cruelty-free cosmetics would be a higher priority if products were easy to identify on the website
  • Customers want to know more about the products they buy before purchasing
  • The checkout process should be easy and clear — confusion causes users to abandon their purchase

Problems and Solutions

Problem 1

Websites failed to display value propositions — users could not quickly identify vegan or cruelty-free products.

Solution

Displayed value propositions prominently on the homepage for immediate recognition.

Problem 2

Ineffective information architecture caused users to abandon their purchase before completion.

Solution

Conducted card sorting and interviews to design a more intuitive architecture with clearly labeled features.

Problem 3

Insufficient product information left customers needing outside help to make purchase decisions.

Solution

Provided comprehensive product information including size, before/after images, and ingredient details.

Problem 4

A complex AI process made users confused and tired, causing them to refuse to use the feature.

Solution

Shortened the AI process and gave it a clearer, more understandable name to reduce friction.

Reflection

What I Learned

  • Encouraging open dialogue and feedback among team members can enhance resilience and prevent burnout.
  • Without understanding the needs and wants of actual users, it is impossible to design a powerful e-commerce experience.
  • A visual style corresponding to the nature of the business helps users understand the website's purpose from the very first glance.
  • Effective communication harnesses diverse perspectives, leading to better decision-making and innovative solutions.

Next step: Design the AI skin guide feature in a way that makes it effortless for users to find products suited to their skin type — the area where users struggled most.
